Lasagna Recipe

This is the classic lasagne alla Bolognese recipe form the Emilia region in Northern Italy.The Bolognese sauce is made with a mixture of beef and pork mince. The addition of prosciutto, red wine, cinnamon and nutmeg make it truly authentic.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 shallot
  • 3 1/2 ounces prosciutto
  • 11 ounces ground pork
  • 11 ounces ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 fluid ounces red wine
  • 21 ounces peeled San Marzano tomatoes

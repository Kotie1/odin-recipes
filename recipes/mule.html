Kombucha Mule Recipe

This tart and fizzy version of the Moscow Mule uses ginger-flavored kombucha instead of ginger beer for a less sweet alternative.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups ice
  • 1/2 fluid ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 4 ounces ginger-flavored Kombucha (such as keVita Mater Brew Ginger)
  • 1 thin lime slice
